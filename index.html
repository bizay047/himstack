About HimStack

A product and service startup building the digital operating system for education.

HimStack was founded on 3 June 2026 by Ritesh Chauhan, Rohit Kumar Shah, Shiv Shankar Yadav, and Abinash Prasad Kurmi. The company combines product engineering, AI/ML research, cloud infrastructure, and service delivery to help educational organizations operate with clarity.

Our mission is to simplify institutional management through secure, intelligent, and accessible technology. Our vision is to become a trusted technology partner for schools, colleges, universities, coaching institutes, and education organizations across emerging markets.
