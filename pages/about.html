About HimStack

Building an intelligent technology company from the ground up.

HimStack is a product and service based technology startup founded on 3 June 2026 by Ritesh Chauhan, Rohit Kumar Shah, Shiv Shankar Yadav, and Abinash Prasad Kurmi.

Company Story

Started with education. Designed to expand.

HimStack began with a clear belief: educational institutions need modern, secure, and easy-to-use software that connects students, parents, staff, and administrators in one digital environment.

The startup combines product development with service delivery, allowing the team to build long-term platforms while also helping organizations solve immediate technology problems through custom software, cloud, AI/ML, web, mobile, and consulting work.

Founder Vision

To make intelligent digital management accessible to institutions that want enterprise-grade systems without unnecessary complexity.

  • Build an AI ERP platform for education
  • Support fast digital transformation for organizations
  • Create a trusted technology brand from emerging markets
  • Keep product quality, security, and customer support central
